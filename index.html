I am a 6th year undergrad student at NTUA School of Architecture. This is my website for ARCH 4342 Computational Methods in Creative Arts and Sciences. As an architecture student, I am interested in exploring how dynamic systems, patterns of decay, and natural processes can inform spatial design. This research allows me to investigate the relationship between geometry, organic transformation, and interactive environments as tools for contemporary architectural expression.
